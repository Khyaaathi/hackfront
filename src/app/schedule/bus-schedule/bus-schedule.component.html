Bus Schedule

The bus stand near the Gundicha Temple provides connections to Bhubaneswar and Cuttack, service every 10-15 minutes. Minibuses to Konark leave every 20-30 minutes . There are direct buses for Kolkata and Visakhapatnam. Mo BUS Service connects Puri to nearby places while OSRTC(Odisha State Road Transport Corporation )connects Puri to far flung areas and other States.
